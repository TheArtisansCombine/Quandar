About Quandar

Our Mission

Quandar was created for those moments when you're faced with a tough decision and just need a little help from fate. It's an easy and fun way to break a deadlock, get a new perspective, or simply add an element of chance to your day.

The Tools We Provide

  • Flip a Coin: For simple, classic choices. You can use the default "Heads" or "Tails," or quickly create a custom coin for options like "Coffee or Tea?" or "Yes or No?"
  • Roll a Die: Go beyond a standard 6-sided die. Our die roller supports various faces (d4, d8, d10, d20) and includes quick picks for popular scenarios like a D&D encounter or a board game roll.
  • Spin a Spinner: Perfect for making a choice from multiple options. Create your own spinner with as many parts as you need, and even apply weights to give certain outcomes a higher chance of being selected.
  • Draw a Card: For those times when you need a choice from a list. Build a deck with your own custom cards, draw a single card, or even split the deck into multiple piles for more complex decisions.

Designed for You

  • Quick & Easy: Our quick-pick options let you jump right into the action with pre-made choices for coins, dice, and spinners.
  • Your Saved Choices: The app remembers your custom configurations, saving them for quick access so you never have to re-enter your favorite coin, die, or deck options.
  • Choose Your Mood: With multiple themes—Natural & Calm, Bold & Energetic, and Modern & Calm—you can customize the app to fit your personal style and mood.

Quandar is your go-to companion for making decisions, big or small. We hope it brings a bit of fun and certainty to your day!
