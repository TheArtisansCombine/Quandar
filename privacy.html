Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how Quandar ("the App") handles user information. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

Quandar is designed to be a client-side application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data about our users. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, IP address, device identifiers, or usage data.

All of your saved content, such as custom coin options, dice, spinners, and card decks, is stored locally on your device using your web browser's local storage. This data is not sent to any server and is not accessible by us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Quandar does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services that collect personal information. Our app is a self-contained tool for your personal use.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
