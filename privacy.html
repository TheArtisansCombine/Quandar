Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 1, 2025

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy as you use the Quandar app. By using our app, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Data Collection & Use

Quandar is designed to be a private, local-first application. We do not collect, use, store, or transmit any personal information about you or your usage of the app. Your privacy is a core feature of Quandar.

2. Data Storage

Any data you create within the app, such as custom coin options, die faces, spinner parts, or card decks, is stored exclusively on your device within your web browser's local storage. This data is not sent to our servers or any third-party servers. If you clear your browser's cache or local storage, this data will be permanently deleted.

3. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently received personal data from a user under the age of 13, we will delete that data immediately. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's online activities.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We advise you to review this policy periodically for any changes.

5. Contact Us
